AMJ expands frozen storage capacity to meet growing market demand

A strategic investment in frozen warehousing strengthens AMJ’s cold-chain capability and supports the growth ambitions of our principals in Qatar.

Frozen warehousing is a critical part of Qatar’s foodservice and FMCG supply chain, especially for principals and customers who depend on product integrity from arrival to delivery.

AMJ’s investment in expanded frozen storage capacity supports greater resilience, broader category coverage and disciplined inventory movement across temperature-sensitive products.

The expansion reinforces AMJ’s wider infrastructure promise: certified facilities, cold-chain control, traceable stock movement and reliable nationwide service.
