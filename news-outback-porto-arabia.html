Outback Steakhouse opens sixth outlet in Qatar at Porto Arabia, The Pearl

The globally renowned Australian-inspired casual dining restaurant continues to delight food lovers in Qatar with its sixth location.

Outback Steakhouse has expanded its Qatar presence with a new outlet at Porto Arabia, The Pearl, strengthening the restaurant portfolio operated by AMJ.

The Australian-inspired casual dining brand is known for grilled-to-order steaks, ribs, seafood, generous hospitality and memorable dining occasions.

The new location supports AMJ’s F&B strategy: bringing globally loved dining concepts closer to Qatar’s communities.
