About AMJ

Serving Qatar
since 1997.

For nearly three decades, AMJ has built a comprehensive food and beverage portfolio supported by modern infrastructure, certified processes and a team committed to service without compromise. Our network serves hypermarkets, supermarkets, hotels, restaurants, catering companies, wholesalers and institutions across the State of Qatar.
