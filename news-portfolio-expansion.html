New brands join the AMJ portfolio across dairy and beverages

AMJ continues to broaden choice for retail and HORECA customers by welcoming new international names into its growing portfolio.

AMJ’s FMCG division is built around quality, consistency and market relevance. Each new brand strengthens the portfolio available to Qatar’s retail, HORECA, wholesale and foodservice channels.

The continued expansion across dairy and beverage categories reflects AMJ’s role as a distribution partner for principals seeking reliable access to Qatar’s market.

With certified infrastructure, cold-chain capability and established customer relationships, AMJ helps world-class brands reach shelves, kitchens and consumers with confidence.
