Est. 1997

A comprehensive portfolio, built to satisfy every food & beverage requirement of Qatar’s most demanding market.

Since 1997, Al Majid Jawad W.L.L has grown from a focused distribution business into a fully integrated operation representing world-class FMCG brands, foodservice solutions and international restaurant concepts across the State of Qatar.

AMJ’s journey has always been shaped by four commitments: the quality of the brands we represent, the reliability of our infrastructure, the strength of our logistics and the professionalism of our people.

Today, AMJ connects the warehouse to the shelf, the kitchen and the dining table. Our portfolio supports principals, retailers, HORECA customers, wholesalers and institutions with service that is dependable, scalable and market-ready.
