Chairman

Ahmed Al Majed

Al Majid Jawad (AMJAD) W.L.L. is a diversified holding company with several commercial interests in the food industry, namely in the Fast-Moving Consumer Goods and Food & Beverage divisions.

The FMCG business started with only a handful of products and evolved into a professional distribution company with diversified portfolio of international food and non-food brands. The fundamental part of our strategy is to work with world-class partners across the globe and offer unique and high-quality products for the discerning taste of the Qatar market.

In addition, wide distribution is our core competence, with our products being available across various trade channels including Supermarkets, Groceries, Convenience stores and Food Service outlets.

We have an exclusive relationship with world-famous restaurant brands such as Dairy Queen Grill & Chill, Outback Steakhouse and Papa John’s Pizza. This division continues to grow with more branches being opened across Qatar making their signature menu offerings available for everyone.

Al Majid Jawad strives to ensure that a balanced approach is taken for all stakeholders, adding value through managerial capabilities and local knowledge, as well as taking significant care to support our country and its people.

We are proud of our past and passionate about our future and look forward to continuing our success alongside each of our partners.

Est. 1997

A comprehensive portfolio, built to satisfy every food & beverage requirement of Qatar’s most demanding market.

Since 1997, Al Majid Jawad W.L.L has grown from a focused distribution business into a fully integrated operation representing world-class FMCG brands, foodservice solutions and international restaurant concepts across the State of Qatar.

AMJ’s journey has always been shaped by four commitments: the quality of the brands we represent, the reliability of our infrastructure, the strength of our logistics and the professionalism of our people.

Today, AMJ connects the warehouse to the shelf, the kitchen and the dining table. Our portfolio supports principals, retailers, HORECA customers, wholesalers and institutions with service that is dependable, scalable and market-ready.
