Al Majid Jawad reinforces commitment to wellness on National Sports Day 2026

AMJ proudly participated in Qatar National Sports Day 2026, reaffirming our commitment to wellbeing, teamwork and healthier communities across our organisation.

National Sports Day reflects values that matter deeply to AMJ: health, teamwork, discipline and shared community spirit.

Our participation in Qatar National Sports Day 2026 brought colleagues together outside the day-to-day rhythm of distribution, warehousing, sales and restaurant operations, reinforcing the people-first culture behind AMJ’s service promise.

As one of Qatar’s active food, beverage and restaurant groups, AMJ continues to support initiatives that encourage wellbeing across our teams and the communities we serve.
